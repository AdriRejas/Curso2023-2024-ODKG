Group 07 Dataset Analysis

Overview of the Dataset

High-level snapshot of the dataset, highlighting its size in terms of rows and columns and offering a quick understanding of the data contained in each column.

  • Size: 385 rows and 9 columns
  • Station Name: The name of the charging station.
  • Street Address: The street address of the charging station.
  • City: The city in which the charging station is located.
  • Access Days Time: The operating hours of the charging station.
  • EV Level1 EVSE Num: The number of Level 1 Electric Vehicle Supply Equipment (EVSE) available at the station.
  • EV Level2 EVSE Num: The number of Level 2 EVSE available at the station.
  • EV DC Fast Count: The number of DC Fast chargers available at the station.
  • EV Other Info: Additional information about other types of chargers or related info.
  • New Georeferenced Column: The geographical coordinates of the charging station.

Quantitative Analysis

Geographical Distribution

The dataset reveals a diverse geographical distribution of charging stations across various cities. Here are the most common cities:

  • Stamford has 21 charging stations.
  • Hartford has 20 charging stations.
  • Fairfield has 20 charging stations.
  • New Haven has 13 charging stations.
  • Danbury has 12 charging stations.

Operational Hours Analysis

The operating hours of the charging stations vary, with some patterns emerging as more common:

  • '24 hours daily' is an operational pattern found in 222 stations.
  • 'Dealership business hours' is an operational pattern found in 26 stations.
  • '24 hours daily; for Tesla use only' is an operational pattern found in 19 stations.
  • '24 hours daily; for customer use only' is an operational pattern found in 17 stations.
  • '24 hours daily; pay lot' is an operational pattern found in 11 stations.

Charger Type Dominance

Among the three types of chargers, Level2 chargers are the most commonly available across the stations.

Stations with Multiple Charger Types

Out of the total stations, 19 stations offer more than one type of charger, showcasing the versatility of these stations.
