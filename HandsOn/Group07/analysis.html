Group 07 Dataset Analysis

Overview of the Dataset

  • Size:
    • Rows (Entries): 385
    • Columns: 9
  • Station Name: The name of the charging station.
  • Street Address: The street address of the charging station.
  • City: The city in which the charging station is located.
  • Access Days Time: The operating hours of the charging station.
  • EV Level1 EVSE Num: The number of Level 1 Electric Vehicle Supply Equipment (EVSE) available at the station.
  • EV Level2 EVSE Num: The number of Level 2 EVSE available at the station.
  • EV DC Fast Count: The number of DC Fast chargers available at the station.
  • EV Other Info: Additional information about other types of chargers or related info.
  • New Georeferenced Column: The geographical coordinates of the charging station.
